Creating Football Enthusiasts

With a ball at your feet and quality turf to play on, what more does an athlete need to fulfill his or her dreams? Great SportsTech Limited (GSL) understands the pain points of its end customers and brings the game-changing technologies specifically engineered for football. Our line of products spans across surface options such as artificial turf, natural grass, and hybrid turf and other stadium solutions such as the turf conditioning system, turf protection system, and modular seating solution.

Our journey of world-class football stadiums across South Asia started in 2004. We have built over 30 FIFA standard football fields and have our footprints across hundreds of educational institutes.

IAAF Standard Athletic Tracks

Great SportsTech Ltd. (GSL) has executed over 40 synthetic athletic and jogging tracks. With our best men on the field, we have designed and built 2 IAAF Class-I Certified tracks and over 14 IAAF Class-II Certified tracks. Not only do we guarantee quality craftsmanship, but we also make sure that the facilities are aesthetically appealing, our blue tracks are a great testimony to that. Various international and national level tournaments including the 35th National Games, Kerala, and African Youth Games have been hosted on our athletic tracks. We have also delivered some key projects beyond the borders like those at the Maryse Justin Stadium, Reduit, Mauritius and Camp Du Roi Stadium, Rodrigues, Mauritius.

From educational institutes to international arenas, GSL has its footprints all over.

Creating Hockey Enthusiasts

Field Hockey is a fast-paced sport that demands proper sport-specific surface performance. Whether your facility is dedicated solely to field hockey or hosts multiple sports, we have a carefully engineered line of products that are ideal for all levels of field hockey. Great SportsTech Ltd. (GSL) is an FIH Certified Field Builder. We have built over 10 hockey fields, that include 8 FIH Certified and 2 FIH standard fields.

We also know how to break the monotony and stand out from the crowd, so we have developed 4 blue hockey fields in the country. Various international and national level hockey tournaments including the Hockey World League Men's Seniors Final - 2015, Raipur and the 35th National Games, Kerala have been hosted on our hockey fields.

Creating Cricket Enthusiasts

In a cricket-loving nation like ours, there are millions who train every day to follow their passion and Great SportsTech Limited (GSL) leaves no stone unturned to help them follow their dreams. We are partnered with the goliaths of the infrastructure industry for cricket and end-customer satisfaction is our only priority. Our line of products spans across various surface options such as artificial turf, which is specifically designed for cricket pitches, and natural grass.

We provide state-of-the-art infrastructure solutions for stadiums that include the soil aeration and vacuum drainage system, modular stadium, floodlights, and much more.

Creating Golf Enthusiasts

Our line of product's meticulously designed features help replicate the exact golfing experience one would have in the best golf courses in the world. Golf courses across India reap the benefits of our golf surfaces in areas that are subject to constant stress such as the tee-off area, practice courses, practice greens, and the driving range.
